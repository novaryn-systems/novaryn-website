AxJedi: Rethinking AX at Scale

What AxJedi Does

  • Faster discovery: Understand exported AX code without weeks of manual review.
  • Impact clarity: See dependencies, risks, and change scope before you commit.
  • Change support: Get draft changes with a human review gate — never auto-apply.
  • Knowledge capture: Build institutional memory across projects and teams.

Why It Matters

AX expertise is scarce. Much of it lives in a few senior consultants' heads. When they leave or get busy, projects stall.

AxJedi gives you a workbench — a thinking partner that knows the codebase. It makes expert time go further. It reduces ramp-up time. It lets junior consultants move faster without losing quality.
